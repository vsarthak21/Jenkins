Is this page still part of my creating/updating my complexity assessment?

No.

Your engagement complexity assessment has been completed and submitted; your engagement complexity assessment has been saved on the engagement complexity database. An engagement complexity assessment report has been generated in a PDF format.

This page has been designed to provide you with the ability to download your engagement complexity assessment report, so that you can print, save and share the details and outcomes from the complexity assessment that you have completed.

Unless your engagement is "Fast Track" you also have an opportunity on this page to download, save and share either or both the weighted and unweighted complexity assessment graphs for your engagement complexity assessment. These have been created in a BitMap format.
