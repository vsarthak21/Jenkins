How do I use this page?

This page has been designed to provide you with the ability to download your engagement complexity report, so that you can print, save and share the report that you have created. Your report has been created in an MS Excel format.

I want to go back to the Home Page

If you press the Home button on the bottom of this page you will remain logged on to the application and you will be returned to the Home Page.

I want to exit from the engagement complexity tool

If you press the Log out link at the top of this page you will be logged out from the application and you will be returned to the Login Page.

Please note: The tab on your browser will remain open. You will need to close the tab manually.
